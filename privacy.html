Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

1. Data Storage & Local Protection

We value your privacy. The application operates primarily offline. All customer records, measurement specifications, work-log tracking, and related tailoring files are processed locally on your device. These details remain in your local workspace sandbox to guarantee maximum privacy and autonomy.

2. Voluntary Cloud Connectivity

Should you explicitly decide to enable real-time coordination or backup features, specific customer indicators are synchronized using secure end-to-end encrypted tunnels. Synchronization profiles are protected via system-enforced authorization rules, and your data remains segregated with strictly private access permissions.

3. Device Permissions & SDK Integrity

We comply strictly with operating system requirements regarding security boundaries:

  • No Hidden Telemetry: We do not track absolute background location, hardware serial identifiers, or dynamic system diagnostic logs.
  • Selective Camera Usage: Camera access is used fully local to translate customer barcodes and check in-store design slips; visual streams are never dispatched to cloud systems unless specifically requested by your actions.
  • Right to Erase: You hold full ownership of your data profiles. You can trigger absolute deletions of any remote profiles instantaneously right through the active dashboard panels.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Data Transfer

Under no circumstances will we sell, assign, rent, or transfer customer records, measurement ledgers, or associated work schedules to marketing brokers, ad distributors, or third-party syndicates. Your files are entirely isolated and protected from broker harvesting.
