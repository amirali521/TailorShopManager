STATEMENT OF SOVEREIGNTY

"Your clients entrust you with their physical proportions to craft garments of exceptional elegant fit. These measurements are not consumer metrics to be harvested or brokered. Under our Offline-First stance, they are stored securely on your active device—empowering your atelier under pure intellectual and data sovereignty."

01 On-Device (Offline-First) Protection

TailorShopManager is designed to run in complete network-isolated containment. Customer identity parameters, design sketches, and waist-to-collar measurements are recorded into an offline sandbox (SQLite/Room engine) directly on your physical device. No telemetry logs or remote telemetry coordinates are extracted.

02 Selective Cloud Synchronization

If you consciously toggle the "Cloud Sync" backup engine, your data is synced using end-to-end encrypted transport layers (SSL) into isolated Firestore document directories. Your database node is protected via standard Rule parameters, guaranteeing no cross-shop access or external data leaks.

03 Compliance with Google Play Core Rules

For users operating our distributed Android APK package:

  • Zero background telemetry: The application does not collect your physical location, device identifiers, or telephone lists.
  • Dynamic permissions override: Camera permissions are used strictly for scanning customer QR tickets and barcodes internally, never transmitted to outside networks.
  • Active Account deletion: In accordance with Play Store developer policies, users can request complete and permanent deletion of their synchronized database profiles instantly via the account ledger screen.

04 Zero-Advertising and Anti-Brokerage Guarantee

We pledge to never sell, rent, disclose, or share customer records, piece-work records, tailoring catalogs, or financial ledgers with advertisement aggregators, consumer tracking brokers, or financial rating agencies. Your customer interactions remain strictly between you and your customer.
