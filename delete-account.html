Account & User Data Deletion Guide

Official compliance instruction sheet • Last Updated: June 28, 2026

1. Overview of Data Ownership

At TailorShopManager, we adhere strictly to user data sovereignty. Any client measurements, tailoring records, employee ledgers, or profile credentials belong entirely to you. You retain absolute control over your personal account status and have the right to request or execute complete erasure of your data at any time.

2. Method A: Instantaneous In-App Deletion

The fastest and most direct route to wipe all of your records is directly inside our Android mobile application:

  1. Launch the TailorShopManager mobile APK on your Android device.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings or Ledger/Profile section.
  3. Select the option labeled "Delete My Account and All Synced Data".
  4. Confirm the prompt. This action immediately deletes your local database workspace sandbox and triggers an absolute, cascading deletion across all connected database endpoints instantly.

3. Method B: Manual Data Deletion Request

If you have uninstalled the application or can no longer access your active device, you can lodge a formal deletion request through our compliance administration.

Please draft an email containing the following detail structure:

  • Subject Line: TailorShopManager - Account Deletion Request
  • Account Email: The registered email address associated with your synchronized profile.
  • Atelier/Shop Name: Registered business coordinates (if applicable).

Dispatch this request directly to our secure inbox: amiralikh.khan02@gmail.com

Note: For security reasons, we will perform validation checks to verify ownership of the account before deletion. Once verified, all cloud records and synchronized buckets will be permanently wiped within 48 to 72 hours.

4. Retained Records & Log Policy

Upon completion of either deletion method, 100% of your customer records, tailor charts, sizing sheets, inventory items, and billing details are permanently and irreversibly purged from our servers. We do not maintain secondary backup copies or shadow records of deleted client data.
